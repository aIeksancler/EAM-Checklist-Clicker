How to use:
1) Download Tampermonkey addon for your browser:
	Firefox:	https://addons.mozilla.org/pl/firefox/addon/tampermonkey/
	Chrome:		https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/tampermonkey/dhdgffkkebhmkfjojejmpbldmpobfkfo
	Edge:		https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/tampermonkey/iikmkjmpaadaobahmlepeloendndfphd

2) Install script: https://github.com/aIeksancler/EAM-Clicker/raw/main/EAM%20Clicker.user.js
3) Use buttons in the to right corner of the EAM website.
